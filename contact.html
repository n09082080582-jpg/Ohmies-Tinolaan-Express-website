About Ohmies Tinolaan Express

Founded in 2020, Ohmies Tinolaan Express was born from a simple mission: to bring the comforting taste of home-cooked Filipino meals to busy individuals and families across Cebu City. Our founder, a passionate home cook and tinola enthusiast, started by sharing her signature chicken tinola with neighbors and friends.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of customers who crave authentic Filipino comfort food. Every dish is prepared using traditional family recipes passed down through generations, combined with the freshest locally-sourced ingredients we can find.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery areas?

We currently deliver to Catbalogan City. Delivery fees vary based on location.

How long does delivery take?

Average delivery time is 30-45 minutes, depending on your location and order volume during peak hours.

Do you offer catering services?

Yes! We offer catering for events, parties, and corporate functions. Contact us for custom packages and pricing.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, GCash, PayMaya, bank transfers, and major credit/debit cards.

Can I customize my order?

Absolutely! We accommodate special requests. Just mention your preferences in the order notes or call us directly.

Do you have vegetarian options?

While our specialty is chicken, we can modify certain dishes upon request. Contact us to discuss options.
